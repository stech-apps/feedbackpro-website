You’ve strategically placed FeedbackPro on Android tablets at key touchpoints throughout your business, capturing valuable in-the-moment insights. But what about gathering feedback from customers after they’ve left your premises, or from those who couldn’t interact with the tablet directly? That’s where FeedbackPro’s multi-channel survey sharing comes in, acting as a powerful extension of your in-location feedback strategy.

## Why Tablet Feedback Alone Isn’t Always Enough

While FeedbackPro’s Android app on strategically placed tablets is fantastic for immediate, on-site feedback, relying solely on this method can create blind spots:

-   **Post-Experience Insights:** Capturing feedback days or weeks after a purchase or service can unlock new insights.
    
-   **Reaching All Customers:** Some customers may not interact with the tablet due to time constraints, accessibility issues, or simply personal preference.
    
-   **Limited Context:** Tablets provide immediate feedback; using additional channels helps you understand “why” behind in-person sentiment.
    

## FeedbackPro’s Expanded Reach: Extending Your Feedback Net Beyond the Tablet

FeedbackPro’s multi-channel sharing options act as a powerful complement to your in-location feedback capture, allowing you to:

-   **Broadcast Surveys to Tablets:** Your strategically placed FeedbackPro Android tablets remain the primary feedback hub.
    
-   **Share Surveys via Email:** Send targeted surveys to customers post-visit for more in-depth feedback on their overall experience.
    
-   **Share Surveys via SMS:** Deliver quick, convenient surveys directly to customers’ phones, perfect for capturing immediate impressions after they leave.
    
-   **Capture Browser-Based Feedback:** Clicking a survey link from an SMS or Email can be done on any device browser, whether phone or computer.
    

Think of it as creating a comprehensive feedback ecosystem, where you can capture insights at every stage of the customer journey.

## The Enhanced Benefits of Multi-Channel Integration

Integrating email and SMS sharing with your tablet-based feedback system unlocks significant advantages:

-   **Complete Customer View:** Gain a holistic understanding of customer sentiment by combining in-location and post-experience feedback.
    
-   **Targeted Follow-Up:** Send personalized surveys to specific customer segments based on their interactions.
    
-   **Increased Data Volume:** Expanding your reach leads to a larger and more representative sample size.
    
-   **Actionable Insights:** Uncover trends and patterns by analyzing feedback from multiple sources.
    

## FeedbackPro: Capturing the Complete Story of Your Customer Experience

We believe in providing you with the tools to gather a comprehensive understanding of your customer experience. FeedbackPro’s multi-channel sharing options, working in tandem with our Android tablet app, empower you to capture insights from every angle, at every stage of the customer journey.
