In today’s fast-paced business environment, real-time customer feedback has become more important than ever. According to a survey by PwC, 32% of customers expect a response within 30 minutes when they contact a company through social media. This means that businesses need to be able to collect and analyze customer feedback in real-time in order to stay competitive.So, what are the benefits of real-time customer feedback? Here are a few:

1.  **Immediate problem resolution:** When you collect feedback in real-time, you can quickly identify and resolve any issues that your customers may be experiencing. You can configure an email notification to be sent to you as soon as an undesired response is received. This can help you improve customer satisfaction and loyalty.
2.  **Faster decision-making:** Real-time feedback allows you to make faster, more informed decisions. Instead of waiting for weekly or monthly reports, you can access up-to-the-minute data and take action immediately.
3.  **Personalized customer experiences:** By collecting real-time feedback, you can better understand your customers’ needs and preferences. This allows you to deliver more personalized experiences, which can lead to increased customer satisfaction and loyalty.
4.  **Competitive advantage:** In today’s digital world, businesses that can respond quickly to customer feedback have a competitive advantage. By collecting and analyzing feedback in real-time, you can stay ahead of the competition and meet your customers’ evolving needs.
