## Why Half-Branded Surveys Miss the Mark

Slapping a logo on a generic-looking survey is like wearing mismatched socks with a power suit. It’s just not a cohesive look. A survey that doesn’t fully embody your brand can:

-   **Feel Disconnected:** Create a jarring experience for your customers, disrupting the flow of your brand narrative.
    
-   **Dilute Brand Recognition:** Fail to capitalize on an opportunity to reinforce your brand identity visually.
    
-   **Undermine Trust:** Look less professional and potentially raise concerns about authenticity.
