### **When Feedback Tablets Sit Idle, Opportunities Are Lost**

Feedback tablets placed in physical locations are designed to capture real customer input—but what happens when no one is using them?  
In many stores, offices, events, and service locations, tablets can sit idle for long periods of time. A blank or static screen not only looks uninviting, it also misses an opportunity to communicate with customers.

That’s where **feedback kiosk idle content** in FeedbackPro adds immediate value.

### **What Is Feedback Kiosk Idle Content in FeedbackPro?**

FeedbackPro allows you to upload **custom images or videos** that automatically display on a feedback tablet when it’s idle for **one minute**. Instead of showing a neutral screen, the tablet becomes a lightweight branding and promotion surface—without interrupting the feedback flow.

This feature is managed directly from the **FeedbackPro dashboard**, under **survey settings**, making it easy to control what customers see when the tablet isn’t in use.

**Supported formats include:**

-   Images: .png, .jpg 
-   Video: .mp4 

Once uploaded, the content appears automatically whenever the tablet is idle, and disappears as soon as a customer interacts with the survey.

### **Why Use Idle Content on Feedback Kiosks?**

Using idle content transforms feedback kiosks from passive devices into active brand touchpoints.

**Key benefits include:**

-   **Stronger branding**Reinforce your logo, colors, and brand message at the exact moment customers are nearby. 
-   **In-store promotion**Highlight current offers, seasonal campaigns, or upcoming events without adding extra signage. 
-   **Clear customer guidance**Use visuals to show customers that the tablet is interactive and ready for feedback. 
-   **Better use of physical space**Every screen in your location works harder, even when no one is submitting feedback. 

Because this content appears only when the tablet is idle, it never interferes with data collection or survey completion.

### **Practical Ways Teams Use Feedback Kiosk Idle Content**

Here are a few common and effective use cases across industries:

-   **Retail locations** display promotional banners or short product videos while customers browse. 
-   **Events and trade shows** showcase sponsor logos or event branding between feedback submissions. 
-   **Hospitality and service businesses** reinforce service values or thank customers for visiting. 
-   **Corporate offices and reception areas** display brand visuals or internal messaging. 

A simple static image can be just as effective as a video—especially when the goal is visibility rather than motion.

### **Tips for Effective Idle Content**

To get the most value from this feature, keep these tips in mind:

-   Use **clear, high-contrast visuals** that are readable from a distance 
-   Keep videos **short and loop-friendly** 
-   Avoid cluttered designs—one message per screen works best 
-   Match visuals to your physical environment and brand tone 

The goal is attraction, not distraction.

### **Turn Idle Time into Brand Impact**

With FeedbackPro’s feedback kiosk idle content feature, idle tablets no longer fade into the background. Instead, they become purposeful, branded touchpoints that support promotions and customer engagement—without compromising feedback collection.
